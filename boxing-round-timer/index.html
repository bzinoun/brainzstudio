Boxing Round Timer is a watchOS app for Boxers, kickboxers, Muay Thai and MMA practitioners, coaches running pad or bag rounds, and home heavy-bag or shadowboxing athletes. It lets you start a full round-and-rest session with one tap, then follow bell and haptic cues without touching the screen.

Features

What it does best

Pick Boxing, MMA, or Muay Thai and the session starts after a short READY countdown. Big full-width buttons, high-contrast color states, and distinct bell and haptic cues for round start, rest, warning, and the final seconds keep you working instead of poking at a screen.

One tap, gloves on

Presets start with a single tap and every control is a large full-width button, so you can pause, resume, or end a session with wraps or gloves on.

Cues you can feel and hear

Distinct haptic patterns and bell sounds mark round start, rest, the warning window, and the final three seconds. Choose haptics, bell, both, or silent.

Your rounds, your rules

Boxing 3 x 3:00, MMA 3 x 5:00, Muay Thai 5 x 3:00, or a custom plan: up to 12 rounds, 1 to 10 minute rounds, 15 second to 3 minute rests, warning at 10 or 30 seconds or off.

Why it matters

The problem it solves

Round athletes lose the flow of a session when the timer lives on a phone across the gym or needs precise taps that gloves and wraps make impossible.

In one minute — You are two rounds into bag work. The phone timer across the room is silent under gym music, so you drift past the round. With Boxing Round Timer, the bell and a wrist tap end the round for you, rest counts down in a different color, and the next round starts itself.

FAQ

Questions, answered

Does Boxing Round Timer work with boxing gloves or wraps on?

Yes. Every control is a large, full-width button sized to be tapped with gloves or hand wraps, and round changes are signaled by bell and haptic cues so you rarely need to look at or touch the screen mid-session.

Which round formats does it support?

One-tap presets for Boxing (3x3:00), MMA (3x5:00) and Muay Thai (5x3:00), plus a Custom mode: 1 to 12 rounds, round lengths from 1:00 to 10:00 and rests from 0:15 to 3:00, in 15-second steps.

Does it run on Apple Watch without my iPhone?

Yes. It runs entirely on Apple Watch (watchOS 10 and later). No phone is needed once a session is started.

What cues does it give during a round?

Distinct bell and haptic cues mark round start, rest, a configurable final-seconds warning (off, 10 or 30 seconds) and session complete. You can choose haptics only, bell only, both, or silent.

How much does it cost?

Boxing Round Timer is free to download for Apple Watch.

Is it a good boxing timer for home heavy-bag and shadowboxing workouts?

Yes. It is built for boxers, coaches running pad or bag rounds, and home heavy-bag or shadowboxing training, with a READY countdown before the first round and automatic rest between rounds.
